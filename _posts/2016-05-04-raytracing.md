__Installation:__

* `git clone https://github.com/wwatkins42/RT.git`
* `cd ./RT`
* `./install.sh`
* `make`

**Usage:**
* `./rt [-s file_path] [-w width] [-h height] [--help]`

**Example:**
* `./rt -s resource/scene/bonus/reflection.yml -w 1920 -h 1080`

**Keys:**
* `esc` quit program.
