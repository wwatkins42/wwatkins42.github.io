_A simple machine learning algorithm to predict cost of rental car given mileage._

__Installation:__

* `git clone https://github.com/wwatkins42/42_C_Projects.git`
* `cd ./42_C_Projects/nm-otool`
* `make`
* `./ft_nm /usr/lib/libATCommandStudioDynamic.dylib`
* `./ft_otool /usr/lib/libATCommandStudioDynamic.dylib`

**Usage:**

nm:
* `./ft_nm files`

otool -t:
* `./ft_otool files`
